Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • slices of cheddar cheese
  • 2 tablespoons of butter

Instructions

  1. Butter one side of each slice of bread
  2. Place a slice of cheese between the slices of bread with the buttered side out
  3. Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat
  4. Place the sandwitch into thr skillet and cook until the bread is golden brown and cheese is melted about 3-4 minutes on each side
  5. Remove from the skillet and let it cool for a minute
  6. Cut the sandwitch in half and serve

Enjoy your delicious homemade grilled cheese sandwich!
